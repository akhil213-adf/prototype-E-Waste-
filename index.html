Sustainability Tips

  • Reduce your use of electronics by upgrading instead of replacing devices.
  • Recycle your electronics properly by taking them to certified e-waste recycling centers.
  • Donate working devices to organizations or individuals in need.
  • Consider buying refurbished devices to reduce new resource consumption.
